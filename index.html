Brief Introduction

India has produced numerous cricketers viz. Lala Amarnath, Sunil Gavaskar, Kapil Dev, Dilip Vengsarkar, Yuvraj Singh, M. S. Dhoni etc. The best cricketers being Sachin Tendulkar and Virat Kohli.

Duleep Singh, Ranji Singh played cricket for England. Sunil Gavaskar was the complete batsman and Kapil Dev the 1st genuine allrounder. Kapil and his team won the 1983 world cup against all odds.
